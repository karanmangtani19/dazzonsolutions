Practice 03

IT Vendor & Security Solution Advisory

Choosing the wrong vendor wastes money and creates problems. We help you define what you actually need, identify the right options, and make a selection based on your environment — not on what a vendor wants to sell you.

Our approach:

  • Define requirements based on actual risk and business needs
  • Identify relevant vendor categories — not every tool in the market
  • Evaluate solutions against architecture fit, scalability, and cost
  • Support vendor shortlisting and decision-making

The focus is on helping you avoid over-buying, reduce complexity, and choose solutions that genuinely fit your environment — without vendor bias.

The objective is to help organisations make confident, well-structured decisions — avoiding unnecessary spend, reducing complexity, and ensuring long-term fit.
